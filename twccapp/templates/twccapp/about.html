About TWCC

The Tanzania Women Chamber of Commerce (TWCC) is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering women entrepreneurs across Tanzania. Established in 2005, we have grown to become the leading voice for women in business.

Our Mission

To create an enabling environment for women to participate fully in economic activities through advocacy, capacity building, and networking opportunities.

Our Vision

To be the premier organization championing women's economic empowerment and business growth in Tanzania.

Our Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty and ethical conduct in all our dealings.

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and create opportunities for all women in business.

Professionalism

We maintain excellence and competence in all our programs and services.

Innovation

We foster creative solutions to advance women's economic participation.
